Our Journey

2010: Founded

Yala Safari Jeep Tour began with a single jeep, driven by a passion for showcasing Yala’s wildlife.

2015: Expanded to Weheragala

We brought our eco-friendly tours to the serene Weheragala National Park (Yala Block 5).

2020: Conservation Partnership

Partnered with local initiatives to protect Sri Lanka’s wildlife and promote sustainable tourism.

Our Mission

To deliver immersive, eco-friendly safari experiences that connect you with Sri Lanka’s wildlife while supporting conservation efforts in parks like Weheragala and Udawalawe.
