Our Journey

2010: Founded with Passion

Yala Safari Jeep Tour was established with a vision to share Sri Lanka’s wild beauty, starting with our first tours in Yala National Park.

2015: Expanding Horizons

We expanded to Udawalawe, Bundala, and Weheragala, offering diverse safari experiences and earning recognition for excellence.

2020: Commitment to Conservation

Partnered with local conservation initiatives to protect wildlife habitats and promote sustainable tourism practices.

2025: Global Trust

Celebrating 15 years of delivering unforgettable adventures to thousands of guests from around the world.
